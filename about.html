LogicMind
About LogicMind
LogicMind is a logic game that tests your ability to solve sequences while still having fun.
Game Modes
Try out any of the game modes of your choice. (Slip-watch)...find the missing number in the sequence before the clock runs out in each interval. (Time run)...find the missing number in as most sequence as you can go within a limit of time.
Highscores
Beating the highscores ain't gonna be easy, but you're free to try!
Coins
You earn coins from your points at the end of a game sessions. Coins are what you use in purchasing tokens for the game. $5 is automatically given to you for every 5 hours you spend on the spend.
Tokens
Coins won during game sessions can be used to buy tokens which helps in unlocking levels, buying time, points and so on.
Answer peek
With answer peek the missing number in a sequence will be reviewed to you, but it comes at a price (sometimes), or free.
Answer peeks are tokens that cannot be bought but earned. They are earned for every 5 hours you spend on LogicMind, if you make 60 points in a game in any mode, for every token you buy and if you make a highscore.
